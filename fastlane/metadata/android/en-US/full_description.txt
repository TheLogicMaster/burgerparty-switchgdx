You just opened a burger restaurant, let's see if you can put together burgers fast enough to satisfy your ever growing number of customers!

As you progress through the levels, unlock new items and visit new places! You start in the USA, but Burger Party will also travel you to a pirate hideout and to Japan. Each world features exclusive items! 

Burger Party also comes with many achievements and a "create your burger" mode which lets you build super high burgers, taking advantage of all the unlocked items.
